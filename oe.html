Operational Excellence

Operational Excellence (OE) is a management of;

  • Safety
  • Health
  • Environment
  • Reliability
  • Efficiency

This management is set to achieve world-class performance. A KSP culture based on "zero incidents and injuries".

In KSP, we;

  • We do it safely or not at all
  • There is always time to do it right
  • One Team One Voice
  • Everyone has the right to go to work and come home safely
